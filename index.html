My favorite taco spots (thus far)

  • Antique Taco in Chicago, Illinois -their chicken al pastor tacos with a margarita never disapoint
  • Birreria Diaz in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma - my fave from here are their bistek aka asada tacos because it takes me back to my hometown as they make them the same way/style
  • La Birria SuPerb in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma -the best birria food truck in the city, can never go wrong!
  • Tony's Tacos in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma -solid and consistent, think if Sonic had a taco place this would be it
  • Torchy's Tacos in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma -very different but super tasty
